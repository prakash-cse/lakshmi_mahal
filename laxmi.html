WELCOME TO

LAKSHMI MAHAL

Elegant, Aesthetically Designed Marriage and Party Hall

Lakshmi Mahal, situated in Devakottai, is a sophisticated and recently constructed venue equipped with modern amenities and central air conditioning. Well-placed lighting fixtures highlight the facade, ensuring that the beauty of the marriage hall is equally mesmerizing during both the day and night. The carefully selected lighting elements create a warm and welcoming atmosphere, setting the tone for a memorable event. Spanning across an extensive 1.20-acre campus, the Mahal boasts impressive architecture, making it a perfect choice for various occasions such as weddings, family gatherings, corporate events, and commercial functions.

The proprietor, Senthilnathan, not only owns the Mahal but also manages and oversees its operations. Clients have the freedom to choose their preferred caterer and other service providers for their functions and events. However, if needed, the Mahal can suggest suitable service providers to meet the specific requirements of the customers. Nonetheless, clients retain the flexibility to directly engage with these service providers for their events and functions.

Approaching the marriage hall, you're greeted by an elegant facade exuding sophistication, adorned with intricate detailing and tasteful embellishments.

MAIN ENTRANCE

The main entrance is adorned with beautifully crafted doors, showcasing a blend of traditional and modern design elements.

TEMPLE AT SITE

RECEPTION HALL

Basic & Additional Services

Mahal Hiring Options

Guests can hire the Mahal for 6 Hours and Multiples with the following options:

  • Entire Mahal with all Facilities
  • 750+ Seating Capacity in the Function Hall
  • 350+ Seating Capacity in the Dining
  • 1,200 Guests (Floating).

Basic services included in the mahal

Following facilities are included in the Basic Mahal Tariff:

  • Centralised Hall with a Grand Event Stage
  • Chairs for the Event
  • Chairs and Tables for Dining
  • Two A/C fully furnished Rooms for Bride and Groom
  • Two fully furnished Rooms for Guests
  • Modern Kitchen with State of Art Facilities
  • Cooking and Serving Vessels
  • Open Car Park inside the Premises to park around 120 Cars
  • Spacious Lawn in Front of the Mahal
